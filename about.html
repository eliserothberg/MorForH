MetroOrDrive
About

    MetroOrDrive came to be because of a five week coding workshop, Coding For Product (the brainchild of the awesome Wai-Yin Kwan), in coordination with Philosophie and Women Who Code. This amazing workshop walked coders of various skill levels through the app cycle from idea, to design, language and framework choices, user testing, and all the way to deployment.

    We focused around advocating the LA Metro system, and the teams came up with some pretty wonderful and varied apps.

    Our team created a tool that computes someone's annual average commute in both time and cost to and from work, by both public transportation and driving so a side-by-side comparison can be made.

    With this tool, commuters can decide which makes the most sense for them: Metro or drive?
